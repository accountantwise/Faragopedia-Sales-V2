## Overview

Farago Projects produced the **Holiday Edit campaign** for Alexander McQueen's Spring/Summer 2026 Pre-Collection, set against the art deco grandeur of **Eltham Palace** in London. The production brought together a large, multi-disciplinary creative team across photography, video, styling, casting, and set design.

The project captures "formality liberated from tradition" — deep burgundy velvets, cobweb lace, jewelled collars, and the house's signature skull motifs, mixed with tuxedo tailoring and crystal embellishments.

## Concept & Theme

Shot at **Eltham Palace** — the iconic 1930s art deco mansion in South-East London — the campaign is framed as "the life of the party" meeting "the charged transition from street to clandestine revelry." Key pieces include:

- Deep burgundy velvet and satin dresses with jewelled collars
- Vibrant red cobweb lace
- The **Knuckle Clutch**, crystal-studded for celebration
- The **metallic Skull Flower bag** and festive satin sandals with crystal bows
- Asymmetric **'MacQueen' tartan** tuxedo tailoring
- The **Manta bag**, a sculptural reimagining of an archival silhouette, decorated with crystal chandelier charms
- Crystal-embellished knitwear
