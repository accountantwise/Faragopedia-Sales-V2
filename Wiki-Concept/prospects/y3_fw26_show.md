## Overview

Farago Projects produced the **Y-3 Fall/Winter 2026 show** — one of the most talked-about presentations of Paris Men's Fashion Week 2026. Staged on **24 January 2026** at the **Palais d'Iéna**, the show was conceived not as a traditional runway but as an **immersive, kinetic performance**, fusing Yohji Yamamoto's avant-garde design language with high-octane collaborations in motorsport, street culture, and fine art.

## Collection Highlights

The FW26 collection was built around dramatic contrasts and high-energy references:

- **Monochromatic black** and oversized silhouettes remained central to the Yamamoto vocabulary
- **Reconstructed garments** and asymmetric tailoring demonstrated the house's signature surrealism
- A major **Mercedes-AMG PETRONAS Formula One** collaboration introduced motocross-inspired technical wear and motorsport motifs
- **Graffiti art by Chito** was applied across key pieces, including a standout leather bomber jacket
- **Skate-culture references** woven through silhouettes and outerwear
- New **adidas Y-3 footwear** silhouettes debuted, pushing performance-led contemporary sneaker design

## Show Format

The presentation used **custom-built ramps and a racetrack-inspired scenography**, with dancers and models moving through a kinetic, choreographed space exploring themes of **velocity, tension, and precision**. The result was a show that felt closer to a live art event than a traditional fashion presentation.

## Special Moments

> Legendary skateboarder **Mark Gonzales** walked the show, modelling a leather bomber jacket bearing **Chito's** signature graffiti artwork — one of the most photographed and celebrated moments of Paris Men's Fashion Week 2026.

## Production Notes

Farago Projects managed the end-to-end event production for this complex, multi-element presentation. The scope included:

- Technical setup for the racetrack-style venue
- Choreography and movement coordination
- Sound, lighting, and staging logistics
- Backstage operations for a large, multi-discipline cast of models and performers
- International press and buyer experience management

This production demonstrated Farago's capacity to handle high-concept, experiential fashion events at the highest level of the industry calendar.
