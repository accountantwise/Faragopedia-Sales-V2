## Overview

The September 2025 issue of *American Vogue* — traditionally the industry's most anticipated issue of the year — featured Academy Award-winning actress **Emma Stone** across its cover and accompanying editorial. The concept was extraordinary: Louis Vuitton Artistic Director **Nicolas Ghesquière** designed a series of custom couture looks for Stone, each dressing her as a different character. The result was a multi-look, cinematic editorial that showcased six distinct personas through Ghesquière's lens.

Photography was handled by **Jamie Hawkesworth**, whose soft, naturalistic style brought an intimacy and warmth to the high-fashion concepts. The fashion editing/styling was overseen by legendary *Vogue* creative force **Grace Coddington**.

## Concept & Theme

The editorial centred on Emma Stone embodying **six distinct characters**, each dressed in bespoke couture pieces created exclusively for the shoot by Nicolas Ghesquière. The project explored Stone's versatility as a performer and collaborator, blurring the line between fashion editorial and cinematic storytelling.

## Production Notes

Farago Projects provided full-scale production management for this flagship September issue shoot. The scale and prestige of the project — involving custom couture, one of fashion's most celebrated photographers, legendary editorial direction, and a major celebrity subject — required sophisticated international logistics, casting coordination, and seamless on-set management. This production represents one of the highest-profile editorial commissions in recent Farago history.
