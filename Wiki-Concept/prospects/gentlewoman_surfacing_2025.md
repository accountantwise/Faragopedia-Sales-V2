## Overview

"**Surfacing**" is a fashion editorial produced for *The Gentlewoman* magazine's **Autumn/Winter 2025 issue**, photographed by **Marius Uhlig**. *The Gentlewoman* is one of fashion's most respected independent publications, known for its intelligent, portrait-driven approach to women in culture — making any Farago-produced credit within its pages a significant distinction.

Marius Uhlig is a photographer with a previous relationship with the publication (he contributed to issues 22 and 28), making this a returning collaboration.

## About The Gentlewoman

*The Gentlewoman* publishes biannually from London. Its editorial ethos prioritises intelligent, considered fashion storytelling over spectacle. Each issue centres on a major cover subject — typically an artist, actor, writer, or cultural figure — with accompanying fashion stories that prioritise character over trend. The publication's high production values and selective, invitation-only approach to advertising give each editorial story particular weight.

## About Marius Uhlig

Marius Uhlig is a German-born photographer working at the intersection of fashion and fine art. Known for restrained, finely-composed images with a strong conceptual underpinning, he has contributed to publications including *D la Repubblica*, *The Gentlewoman*, and various international fashion titles.
