## Overview

"**Onward in Timeless**" is a fashion editorial published in *W Magazine China* Issue #19 (February 2026), photographed by **Mel Bles**. The story explores themes of enduring elegance, understated luxury, and the relationship between contemporary fashion and timeless style conventions.

## Concept & Theme

The editorial's title — "Onward in Timeless" — speaks to an aesthetic philosophy of moving forward while honouring the classic. Mel Bles' photography is typically characterised by high-contrast lighting, cinematic framing, and sculptural compositions. The editorial presents Frauke Nijs and Kris Krystal in a curated selection of high-fashion pieces aligned with this editorial vision.

## About Mel Bles

Mel Bles is a Dutch-born, internationally active fashion and beauty photographer whose client list includes major luxury brands and publications worldwide. His work is distinguished by its visual precision and narrative depth, making him a natural fit for an editorial centred on timelessness.

## Production Notes

Farago Projects coordinated international talent and team logistics for this production. The shoot required nuanced, precise execution to serve the specific aesthetic demands of *W Magazine China*'s editorial vision for their February issue, one of the key issues ahead of the Chinese New Year period.
