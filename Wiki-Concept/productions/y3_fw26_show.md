## Overview

Full-scale event production for the **Y-3 Fall/Winter 2026** runway show. Y-3, the collaboration between Adidas and designer Yohji Yamamoto, celebrated its FW26 collection with a high-energy presentation managed and executed by Farago Projects.

## Creative Credits

- **Creative Direction:** Yohji Yamamoto

## Production Notes

Farago Projects was responsible for the end-to-end execution of the show, including technical setup, sound design, lighting choreography, and backstage management. The production captured the utilitarian yet avant-garde spirit of the FW26 collection, ensuring a seamless experience for global press, buyers, and industry influencers.
