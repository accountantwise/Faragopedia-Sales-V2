## Overview

A major campaign and lookbook production for **Alexander McQueen**, showcasing the Spring/Summer 2026 Pre-Collection and the 2025 Holiday Edit. The visuals continue the house’s tradition of blending romanticism with sharp tailoring and dark, evocative imagery.

## Creative Credits

- **Photographer:** Sam Khoury

## Production Notes

Produced by Farago Projects in late 2025, the shoot required high-end location management and set design to match the sophisticated aesthetic of the McQueen brand. The campaign was distributed globally across digital and print media as part of the brand's seasonal holiday push.
