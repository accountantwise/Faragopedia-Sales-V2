## Overview

"**In The Dark, The Tides Shine Bright**" is an experimental creative project and show design produced by Farago Projects in collaboration with artist Jeano Edwards. The project explores atmospheric visuals and immersive experiences, pushing the boundaries of traditional production.

## Creative Credits

- **Artist/Director:** Jeano Edwards
- **Production & Management:** Farago Projects

## Production Notes

This project highlights Farago Projects' expansion into multidisciplinary artistic ventures. The production involved complex technical coordination, art buying, and project management to bring Edwards' vision to life, blending emotional storytelling with high-production value aesthetics.
