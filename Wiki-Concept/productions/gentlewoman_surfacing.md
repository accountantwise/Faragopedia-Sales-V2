## Overview

The "**Surfacing**" editorial was a featured story in the Autumn/Winter 2025 edition of *The Gentlewoman*. The project highlights the magazine's signature focus on modern, intelligent women, presented through a minimalist and architecturally-focused visual lens.

## Creative Credits

- **Photographer:** Marius Uhlig
- **Production:** Farago Projects
- **Art Direction:** Farago Projects / Marius Uhlig

## Production Notes

Farago Projects collaborated with photographer Marius Uhlig to produce a shoot that emphasized clean lines and structured silhouettes. The production involved meticulous attention to location scouting and lighting design to achieve the "Surfacing" theme's atmospheric and evocative qualities.
