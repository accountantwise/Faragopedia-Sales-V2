# Farago Projects — Company Summary

**Farago Projects** is a creative production company specializing in full-scale production services for the fashion, beauty, and luxury industries. The company is based in London and Paris, and produces stills, videos, and projects worldwide. They also maintain offices in New York, Los Angeles, and Rio de Janeiro, and their services extend to creative production, events, and special projects.

---

## Founder & Origins

The company was founded by **Sylvia Farago**, who serves as Producer and Owner. She has held that role since September 2012, and previously co-founded Simmonds Ltd (2010–2012), worked as a Creative Media Producer at Burberry (2009–2010), and served as Photographic Director and then Photographic Editor at *Dazed & Confused* magazine (2002–2008). The company was formally known as Sylvia Farago LTD before rebranding.

---

## Legal & Corporate Details

Farago Projects LTD is a private limited company incorporated on 13 August 2012, registered in London at Suite 2a, 7th Floor, City Reach, 5 Greenwich View Place, E14 9NN. The company's status is active.

There is also a separate French entity — Farago Projects SAS, founded in 2019, with its line of business in services independent of motion picture production. A U.S. entity, Farago Projects LLC, is registered in New York.

---

## Services

Farago Projects is a creative production company producing stills and motion, including scout, casting, and post-production management. Their tagline describes them as "providing full-scale production services worldwide."
