# Custom GPT

User-built GPT models tailored for specific tasks or workflows within ChatGPT.

## Details

Custom GPTs can be created to optimize prompts for specific tools or automate workflows. For example, a GPT can be built to generate video prompts for tools like Runway Gen-3. Multiple GPTs can be chained together in one chat for more complex tasks. They can also be used for onboarding new employees by converting Loom video transcripts into SOPs.
