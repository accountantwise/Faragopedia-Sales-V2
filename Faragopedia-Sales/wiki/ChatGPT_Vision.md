# ChatGPT Vision

A feature that allows users to upload images such as charts or graphs for ChatGPT to analyze and explain.

## Details

Users can upload charts, graphs, or screenshots and ask for explanations. An example prompt is: 'Explain this Venn diagram about AI vs. Machine Learning in simple terms.' A hot tip is to request explanations at multiple comprehension levels, such as ages 5, 10, 20, and PhD level.
