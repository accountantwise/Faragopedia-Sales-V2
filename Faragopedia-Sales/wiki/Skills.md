## Details

Two types of skills exist: pre-built Anthropic skills (document handling for Excel, PowerPoint, Word, PDF) and custom skills (user-defined domain-specific workflows and context). Skills are versioned and can be pinned to a specific version or set to 'latest.' A maximum of 20 skills per session is supported. Skills are loaded on demand, meaning they only consume context window space when the agent actually needs them.
