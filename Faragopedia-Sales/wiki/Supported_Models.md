# Supported Models

All Claude 4.5 and later models are supported in Managed Agents, with different models suited to different task complexity and cost requirements.

## Details

Three primary models: Opus 4.6 (claude-opus-4-6) for complex reasoning and highest quality; Sonnet 4.6 (claude-sonnet-4-6) as the default and best balance of speed and intelligence; Haiku 4.5 (claude-haiku-4-5-20251001) for fast, cheap, simple tasks. Recommended routing strategy: use Haiku for summarization and formatting, Sonnet for most work, and Opus for hard problems. Separate agents can be created for each model and tasks routed accordingly.
