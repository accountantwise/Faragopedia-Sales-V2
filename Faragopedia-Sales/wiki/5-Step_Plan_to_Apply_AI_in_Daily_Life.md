# 5-Step Plan to Apply AI in Daily Life

A structured approach to integrating AI tools like ChatGPT into everyday workflows.

## Details

The five steps are: (1) Set a goal or problem to solve; (2) Choose a relevant tool; (3) Use trial-and-error or follow a tutorial; (4) Apply the tool to a real-world task; (5) Reflect on the process and results, remaining open to learning new workflows.
