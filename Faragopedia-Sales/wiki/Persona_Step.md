# Persona Step

The fourth step in the prompt formula, which involves assigning a specific role or expertise to ChatGPT.

## Details

Persona allows ChatGPT to embody a specific character or area of expertise, such as a physical therapist for injury rehab programs or a Fortune 500 hiring manager for job interview preparation. A pro tip is to prompt ChatGPT to respond in the style of a famous individual, such as 'Rewrite this blog post in the style of Eminem.'
