# Job Search Optimization Use Case

A ChatGPT use case focused on improving resumes and cover letters for specific job applications.

## Details

Users can upload their resume and a job description, then prompt ChatGPT to review and suggest improvements by matching the resume against the job description. A follow-up prompt can generate a tailored cover letter. Example prompt: 'I'm applying for a marketing manager role. Here's the job description. Review my resume and suggest improvements by matching it against the JD.'
