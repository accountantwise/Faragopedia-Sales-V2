# ChatGPT

An AI chatbot developed by OpenAI for natural language understanding and generation.

## Details

Launched in November 2022 by OpenAI, ChatGPT builds on the GPT (Generative Pre-trained Transformer) family of large language models. Notable versions include GPT-3.5, GPT-4, GPT-4-turbo, and GPT-4o. It is useful for tasks like writing, research, coding, translation, text summary, education, data analysis, and personal productivity. It is accessible at chatgpt.com and available on desktop (macOS), Android, and iOS platforms.
