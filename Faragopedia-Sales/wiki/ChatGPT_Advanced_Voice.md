# ChatGPT Advanced Voice

A real-time voice conversation feature in ChatGPT for interactive and low-latency spoken communication.

## Details

This feature enables users to speak directly to ChatGPT with low latency, making it ideal for language learning. An example prompt is: 'You are a native French speaker. Help me practice ordering food at a restaurant in French.' Users can also ask ChatGPT to adjust the speed of responses for more tailored practice.
